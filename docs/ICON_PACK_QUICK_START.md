# Icon Pack Quick Start Guide

Get custom icons on your Járngreipr launcher in 3 easy steps! 🎨

## Step 1: Install an Icon Pack

Download a free icon pack from the Play Store. Here are some great options:

### Recommended Icon Packs

| Icon Pack | Style | Best For |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| **Whicons** | White minimalist | Clean, simple look |
| **Flight Lite** | Colorful flat | Fun, vibrant style |
| **Lines Free** | Line art | Modern, minimalist |
| **Delta** | Adaptive | Material Design |
| **Urmun** | Material icons | Google-style apps |

**How to install:**

1. Open Play Store
2. Search for an icon pack (e.g., "Whicons")
3. Tap **Install**
4. Wait for installation to complete

## Step 2: Apply the Icon Pack

1. Open **Járngreipr** launcher
2. Tap the **⚙️ Settings** icon (in the search bar)
3. Scroll to **Icon Pack** (under Appearance section)
4. Tap to expand the list
5. Select your installed icon pack
6. Wait a moment while icons load
7. Tap the back button

**That's it!** Your app icons should now match your icon pack style! 🎉

## Step 3: Enjoy Your New Look

Swipe through your apps to see the new icons.

**Notes:**

- Some apps may still show default icons if they're not included in the icon pack
- This is normal - not every icon pack includes every app
- You can always switch to a different icon pack or reset to default icons

## Switching Back to Default

Want to go back to original icons?

1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **Icon Pack**
3. Select **Default Icons** at the top
4. Done! ✨

## Troubleshooting

### Icons didn't change?

- **Wait a moment**: Icon loading can take a few seconds
- **Go back and return**: Navigate away from the home screen and come back
- **Try another icon pack**: Some icon packs may have compatibility issues

### Only some icons changed?

- **This is normal!** Icon packs don't always include every app
- Apps without custom icons will show their original icon
- Popular apps (Chrome, Gmail, etc.) are usually included

### Icon pack not showing in list?

- **Make sure it's installed**: Check in your app drawer
- **Restart the launcher**: Close and reopen Járngreipr
- **Check compatibility**: Not all "icon packs" work with launchers - make sure it's a launcher icon
  pack

## Finding More Icon Packs

Search the Play Store for:

- "icon pack"
- "launcher icons"
- "custom icons"

Look for packs that mention:

- Nova Launcher
- ADW Launcher
- Apex Launcher
- Launcher compatibility

These are most likely to work with Járngreipr!

## Pro Tips

### Mix and Match

Try different icon packs to find your favorite style:

- **Minimalist**: Whicons, Lines, Ameixa
- **Colorful**: Flight, Urmun, Moonshine
- **Material**: Delta, Rundo, Rugos
- **Dark**: Glasklart, Bleach, Dark

### Theme Your Device

Coordinate your icon pack with:

- Járngreipr color theme
- Custom wallpaper
- System accent colors

### Performance

- First time applying an icon pack may take a few seconds
- After that, icons load instantly (they're cached!)
- Switching between packs is fast
